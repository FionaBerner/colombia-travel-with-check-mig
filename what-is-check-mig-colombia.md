# 🛂 What Is Check‑MIG Colombia?

[Check‑MIG Colombia](https://checkmigcolombia.us/) is a mandatory online pre‑registration form for all foreign travelers entering or leaving the country. It is designed to streamline border control procedures and reduce processing time at immigration checkpoints.

## Purpose of Check‑MIG
- Helps Colombian immigration authorities verify traveler information in advance.
- Reduces waiting times at airports and land borders.
- Ensures smoother entry for tourists, business travelers, and returning residents.
- Supports health and security protocols.

## When to Complete the Form
- The form must be completed **before arrival** or **before departure**.
- Travelers typically fill it out **24 hours to 1 hour** before their flight.
- A confirmation page or email is generated after submission.

## What Information You Need
- Passport details.
- Flight number and travel itinerary.
- Accommodation address in Colombia.
- Basic personal information.

## What Happens After Submission
- You receive a confirmation (PDF or email).
- Immigration officers may request to see it upon arrival.
- Keep it accessible on your phone or printed.

## Important Reminder
Foreign visitors must complete the **mandatory online Check‑MIG form** before traveling to Colombia. This online form is required for smooth entry into the country.
