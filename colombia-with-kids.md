# 👨‍👩‍👧 Traveling to Colombia With Kids

Colombia is a family‑friendly destination with diverse landscapes, cultural experiences, and activities suitable for children of all ages. With proper planning, families can enjoy a safe and enriching trip.

## Health and Vaccination Considerations
Routine childhood vaccinations should be up to date. Additional considerations:
- **Yellow fever vaccine** is recommended for certain regions (Amazon, some jungle areas).
- **Mosquito protection** is important in tropical zones.
- **Altitude awareness** is necessary in Bogotá and other high‑altitude cities.
- **Hydration** is essential in warm coastal areas.

For personalized medical guidance, consult a healthcare professional before traveling.

## Activities for Kids and Teenagers
Colombia offers a wide range of family‑friendly experiences:
- **Bogotá:** Gold Museum, Monserrate cable car, botanical gardens.
- **Medellín:** Parque Explora, cable cars, interactive science museums.
- **Cartagena:** Beaches, boat trips, Castillo San Felipe fortress.
- **Coffee Region:** Coffee farm tours, butterfly gardens, nature parks.
- **San Andrés:** Calm beaches, snorkeling, glass‑bottom boat tours.

Teenagers often enjoy:
- Ziplining and adventure parks.
- Hiking in national parks.
- Cultural tours and street art walks.
- Surfing lessons on the Pacific coast.

## Tips for Traveling With Kids
- Choose accommodations with family‑friendly amenities.
- Plan activities early in the day to avoid heat.
- Use car seats or boosters when traveling by taxi or rental car.
- Keep snacks, water, and sun protection handy.
- Allow time for rest between excursions.

## Emergency Phone Numbers
- **Police:** 123  
- **Ambulance:** 123  
- **Fire:** 119  

## Traveling With Dogs
Colombia allows entry for dogs, but regulations apply:
- A veterinary health certificate is required.
- Proof of rabies vaccination is mandatory.
- Some airlines require additional documentation.
- Pets may be inspected upon arrival by Colombian authorities.

Check airline policies and Colombia’s agricultural health agency requirements before traveling.

## Important Reminder
Foreign visitors must complete the [Colombia Check‑MIG](https://checkmigcolombia.us/) form before traveling to Colombia. This online form is required for smooth entry into the country.
