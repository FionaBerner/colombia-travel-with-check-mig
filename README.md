# 🧭 Colombia Travel With Check‑MIG — Your Guide to a Smooth Arrival

Foreign travelers visiting Colombia must complete the **Check‑MIG Colombia** form before entering or leaving the country. This simple online pre‑registration helps streamline immigration procedures and ensures a faster, smoother arrival at Colombian airports and land borders. This repository provides clear, practical information to help you understand the Check‑MIG process and prepare for your trip to Colombia.

## Why Check‑MIG Colombia Matters?
- It is a **mandatory** requirement for all foreign visitors.
- It speeds up immigration processing.
- It reduces paperwork and waiting times at the airport.
- It ensures your travel information is pre‑verified before arrival.

## How Check‑MIG Works?
- The form is completed online before your flight.
- Travelers typically fill it out **24 hours to 1 hour** before departure.
- After submission, you receive a confirmation that immigration officers may request to see.
- Keep the confirmation accessible on your phone or printed.
